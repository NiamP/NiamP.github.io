The Growth of ESG Investment and its Impact

Aims

This project aims to explore the growth and impact of ESG investment practises on markets, companies, and the borader world. The rise in global temperatures and the escalating effects of climate change have driven market participants to prioritise investments that align with ethical and sustainable values. Over the past decade, ESG investing has become a highly discussed trend among capital allocators and this project seeks to examine how ESG investing has evolved, whether there are characteristics which determine a companies likelyhood of having a strong ESG performance, and evaluate whether this increasing emphasis on ESG truly influences the world in a meaningful way.
